Potato leaf disease detection

The project utilizes Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to automate the identification and diagnosis of diseases affecting potato leaves. By leveraging CNNs, the system can analyze images of potato leaves and accurately classify them based on the presence or absence of diseases such as blight, late blight, or early blight. This automated approach streamlines the disease detection process, enabling farmers and agricultural professionals to quickly identify diseased plants and take timely preventive measures. .

Extractive text summarization

The project focused on implementing and comparing two extractive text summarization algorithms: TextRank and Frequency-Based summarization. Both algorithms achieved perfect recall, indicating their ability to capture all relevant information. However, the Frequency-Based method outperformed TextRank in terms of precision and F1 Score, suggesting better sentence selection for the summary. The choice between the two algorithms depends on specific project requirements and the desired balance between precision and recall.
